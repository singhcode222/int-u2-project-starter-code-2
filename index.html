Save The Bees! 🐝


Bees are some of the most important pollinators in our ecosystem. Many plants and animals depend on them for survival. However, many bee species are facing threats such as habitat loss, pesticides, and climate change. It is crucial that we take action to protect and conserve bee populations to ensure the health of our environment and food supply.

Problem 3: Climate Change

Climate change alters flowering times and reduces the availability of floral resources, disrupting bee foraging and reproduction. Increased temperatures and extreme weather also destroy nesting sites and spread pests and diseases, further threatening bee populations.
